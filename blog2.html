📘 Full Syllabus

CHS exam ka syllabus mainly 3 sections me divided hota hai: Mathematics, Science aur Reasoning. Mathematics me percentage, ratio, algebra aur geometry jaise topics aate hain. Science me Physics, Chemistry aur Biology ke basic concepts cover hote hain. Reasoning section me logical thinking aur problem solving questions aate hain.

Students ko chahiye ki wo NCERT books se preparation kare aur previous year papers solve kare. Regular practice aur mock tests se exam me performance improve hota hai.

📚 Preparation Strategy

Preparation ke liye sabse important hai consistency. Daily 2-3 ghante study karna zaruri hai. Weak subjects pe zyada focus kare aur strong topics ko revise karte rahe. Time management bhi important hai kyunki exam me limited time hota hai.

Mock tests dene se confidence badhta hai aur real exam ka experience milta hai. Har test ke baad analysis kare aur apni mistakes ko improve kare.
